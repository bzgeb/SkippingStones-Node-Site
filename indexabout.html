About the Game

Skipping Stones is a contemplative game about nature and music. In the game players explore a lakeside clearing, where each action they take creates and layers the game's soundscape. Containing a beautifully evocative generative sound system and painterly polygons, Skipping Stones is a game about getting away from it all.
